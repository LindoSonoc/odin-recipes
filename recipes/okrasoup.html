Okra Soup

By: Leendor | Updated on April 28, 2026

Okra Soup is a flavorful African dish often served with pap or nshima. This chunky version is easy to make and packed with delicious fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 500g okra
  • 2 teaspoons bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ripe tomato
  • 1/2 red onion (optional)
  • 2 cups water

Preparation Method : 25 Minutes

  1. Wash and cut the okra into chunky pieces about 2cm long.
  2. Chop the tomato and onion into small pieces.
  3. Boil the water in a medium saucepan and add salt and bicarbonate of soda.
  4. Add vegetables by placing the tomato and onion into the boiling water first.
  5. Add okra after 3 minutes and cook gently without stirring too much.
  6. Cook for 10–15 minutes until the okra softens and the soup thickens slightly.
  7. Serve hot with pap or nshima.
