Pumpkin Leaves

By :Leendor | Updated on April 27 ,2026

Umbhido (pumpkin leaves) is a nutritious, traditional dish made by steaming leaves, stems, and often chopped pumpkin, resulting in a soft, green side dish

Ingrediants

  • Pumpkin leaves: 1 large bunch, thoroughly cleaned.
  • Onion: 1, chopped.
  • Tomato: 1 or 2, diced (optional).
  • Water: 1 cup.
  • Salt: to taste.
  • Optional: 1-2 chopped potatoes, oil/butter, or peanut butter for creamy texture.

Preparation Method : 15 Minutes

  1. Prep the leaves: Remove the tough, fibrous hairs from the stems ("skinning" them) and discard the long, thin tendrils.
  2. Wash: Wash leaves carefully in cold water to remove sand.
  3. Chop: Chop the leaves and tender stems finely.
  4. Wash: Wash leaves carefully in cold water to remove sand.
